Welcome back, neighbours! Effective August 9, 2021, Canada will allow entry to fully vaccinated American citizens and permanent residents (currently residing in the US) for leisure travel. If you meet the entry criteria, fully vaccinated travellers do not have to quarantine upon arrival to Canada. More information.


BC has sustained an extended period of very hot and dry weather conditions. As a result, some areas of the province are currently dealing with wildfires, and a campfire ban is officially in effect for all of BC. British Columbia is a very large and diverse province, and there are many areas that are not directly impacted which are open for business and excited to welcome you. Conditions can change rapidly.
